Privacy Policy

Last Updated: April 18, 2026

The developer of Trash Cycle believes that your data belongs to you. This app was built to be a simple, lightweight utility that respects your privacy.

1. Data Collection

Trash Cycle does not collect, store, or transmit any personal data.

  • No Accounts: You do not need to create an account to use the app.
  • No Tracking: We do not use any third-party analytics, tracking SDKs, or advertising frameworks.
  • No Telemetry: We do not collect information about how you use the app or your system specifications.

2. Local Processing

All app operations—such as monitoring your trash folder and executing the "Empty Trash" command—happen entirely on your Mac. No data ever leaves your device. Your settings (intervals, notification preferences) are stored locally using standard macOS defaults.

3. Third-Party Services

Trash Cycle does not share any information with third parties.

4. Children's Privacy

Because we do not collect any data, our app is safe for users of all ages and does not knowingly or unknowingly collect information from children.
