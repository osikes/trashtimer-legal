Frequently Asked Questions

How do I change the auto-empty interval?

Click the Trash Cycle icon in your menu bar, then select "Preferences" to adjust the interval for automatically emptying your trash.

Can I disable notifications?

Yes! Go to Preferences and toggle off notifications. You can also manage this in System Settings → Notifications → Trash Cycle.

Is my data safe?

Absolutely. Trash Cycle runs entirely on your Mac and never collects or transmits any data. See our Privacy Policy for details.

How do I uninstall Trash Cycle?

Simply drag Trash Cycle from your Applications folder to the Trash. All preferences are stored locally and will be removed with the app.
